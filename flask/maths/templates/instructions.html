INSTRUCTIONS  

Participation Rules

  • The contest is open only for MCA students of India.
  • Only individual participation is allowed.

General Rules and Instructions

  • You'll get 200 points at the start of the event.
  • There are 3 stages in this event, divided on the basis of the questions' difficulties.
  • Topic to which each question is related will be mentioned.
  • You have to bid on each question after seeing the topic mentioned.

    • Bidding System

    Level Minimum Bid Maximum Bid
    Easy 5 25
    Medium 20 50
    Hard 40 100

  • Do not leave any unnecessary spaces before or after any answer.
  • Supported types of answer are mainly integers and decimal(if applicable).
  • Once you skip a question you are not allowed to visit it again.
  • Total number of attempts for each question is 3.
  • Correct Submission will increase your total score by 2 times of bidding point.
  • Wrong submission on 1st and 2nd attempt will decrease your total score by 5 each time and in 3rd attempt it will lead to reduce your points by the bidding point.

Special Rules and Instructions

  • Don't spoil! Don't share how you solved each question with other participants.
  • Any attempt of attack on site is intolerable. The player will be blacklisted and won't be able to participate in any other events.
